## Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
Momentum helps you build deep focus habits by tracking exactly how much time you spend on what matters.

Create focus activities for anything — coding, reading, writing, studying, exercise — set a daily time target, and start tracking. Every session you complete adds to your momentum.

──────────────────────────────
⏱ SMART ACTIVITY TIMERS
──────────────────────────────
• Set individual daily targets for each activity
• Visual progress bar shows how close you are to your goal
• Only one timer runs at a time — switching activities auto-pauses the previous one
• Timer state is saved — close the app and pick up exactly where you left off

──────────────────────────────
🍅 BUILT-IN POMODORO MODE
──────────────────────────────
• Toggle Pomodoro mode on with one tap
• Choose your session length — from 5 to 120 minutes
• Timer auto-pauses after each session with an alarm so you never miss your break
• Every 4 sessions, take a long break — Momentum keeps count
• Choose your own alarm sound from your device's full ringtone library

──────────────────────────────
📊 SESSION HISTORY
──────────────────────────────
• Every completed session is automatically logged
• View your history grouped by day with per-day totals
• See which activities you actually spent time on
• Full history kept locally on your device — no account needed

──────────────────────────────
🎯 BUILT FOR REAL FOCUS
──────────────────────────────
• Drag and drop activities to prioritize your list
• Manage mode for adding, editing, and removing activities
• Custom emoji icons to personalise each activity
• Confetti and haptic feedback when you hit your daily target
• Emergency stop: press the power button to silence any alarm instantly

──────────────────────────────
🔒 PRIVATE BY DESIGN
──────────────────────────────
• No account, no sign-up, no cloud sync
• All data stored locally on your device
• No ads, no tracking, no analytics

──────────────────────────────
✨ CLEAN & MINIMAL
──────────────────────────────
Momentum uses a focused dark interface designed to keep distractions out and your work front and center. Everything you need is one tap away — nothing you don't.

Whether you are trying to hit 2 hours of deep work, finish a reading habit, or stay consistent with daily practice, Momentum gives you the clarity to see your effort and the satisfaction of watching it grow.

Build focus. Build habits. Build momentum.
```
