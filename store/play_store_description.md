## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

**Momentum** is a minimalist focus timer designed to help you build real, lasting habits — one deep work session at a time.

Most productivity apps overwhelm you with streaks, gamification, and endless notifications. Momentum does the opposite: it gets out of your way and lets you focus on what actually matters.

---

### 🎯 How It Works

Create your own activities — anything you want to build a habit around. Set a daily time target. Press play. That's it.

Momentum tracks how long you spend on each activity each day, shows your progress toward your targets, and keeps a complete history of every session so you can see your growth over time.

---

### ✨ Key Features

**Activity Timer**
- Create unlimited custom activities with your own name, emoji icon, and daily target
- Accurate time tracking with a live stopwatch display
- Visual progress bar showing how close you are to your daily goal
- Mark sessions as done or let the timer auto-complete when you hit your target

**Pomodoro Mode**
- Built-in Pomodoro technique support — set your session length (5–120 min)
- Automatic break reminders with alarm sound when each session ends
- Session counter so you always know how many Pomodoros you've completed
- Skip or take breaks at your own pace — no forced timers

**Smart Alarm**
- Distinct alarm sound when you complete a session or Pomodoro
- Choose your own alarm sound from your device's full sound library
- Press the power button to silence the alarm instantly
- Alarm auto-stops after 5 seconds for completed sessions

**Session History**
- Full log of every session you complete
- Searchable by day — see exactly how you spent your time
- Motivates consistency by making your effort visible

**Drag to Reorder**
- Long press any activity card to rearrange your list
- Put your most important activities at the top
- Smooth, intuitive drag-and-drop interface

**Daily Motivation**
- A different motivational quote from world-class thinkers greets you every time you open the app
- 50 hand-curated quotes from James Clear, Cal Newport, Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, and more
- Because the right words at the right moment can change your whole session
